Section One · Eagle Eye

See the hole like a caddie does.

Satellite imagery with live GPS distances to front, center, and back — plus a plays-like number that folds in elevation, wind, and temperature, so 319 reads as the 311 it actually plays.

  • Tap anywhere for exact carry and leave-yourself distances.
  • Club arcs from your bag — overlays built from your real distances, not tour averages.
  • Log shots as you walk — every swing becomes Strokes Gained data.
Section Two · Live Matches

One code. Everybody's card. Live.

Start a match, share a four-letter code, and every phone in the group becomes the same scorecard — leaders, birdies, and blowups updating hole by hole.

  • Stroke, match, and team formats with net scoring off real handicaps.
  • Guests welcome — friends without the app get a row on the card too.
  • Never lose your round — scores survive dead zones and dead batteries.
  • Partial rounds count — quit after 12? Stats only count the holes you played.

Section Three · The Caddie

It knows your bag, your numbers, your leaks.

Ask about a shot, a hole, or your game. Answers come from your measured club distances and your Strokes Gained profile — not generic tips.

  • "165 out, slight uphill, breeze into me — what club?"
  • "Where am I losing strokes right now?"
  • "Build me a 20-minute warm-up for tomorrow's match."
Section Four · Your Game, Measured

A real handicap. Real answers about your game.

Every round — solo or match — feeds a WHS-method Handicap Index, Strokes Gained across your whole game, and a practice plan aimed at the exact place you're bleeding strokes.

  • Handicap Index computed the USGA way: best 8 of your last 20, net double bogey, the works.
  • Strokes Gained off the tee, approach, around the green, and putting.
  • GamePlan — tee-to-green strategy priced for your dispersion, not a pro's.
  • Rivalries — lifetime head-to-head records with every friend you play.
Section Five · Practice & Planning

Your best round starts the night before.

The Match earns its spot in your bag before you ever step on the course. It already knows your game — your misses, your danger holes, your real club distances — and turns that into work that's yours alone.

  • Focus Areas reads your recent rounds and names the biggest leak — then builds a guided weekly practice session with drills, minutes, and check-off targets.
  • Game Day Strategy writes tomorrow's plan tonight: a hole-by-hole read of the course you're playing, calling your clubs off every tee and containing your historical danger holes.
  • Customized to each player — shot shape, typical miss, dispersion, tendencies. No two golfers get the same plan, because no two golfers play the same game.

Section Seven · The Tour

The pros, live, one tab away.

The Tour tab carries live professional leaderboards right next to your own card — position, score, today's round, and holes thru, refreshing while you watch.

  • Live PGA Tour scores — round-by-round leaderboards with a live badge and auto-refresh.
  • Every event of the week — switch between concurrent tournaments with one tap.
  • Player cards — headshots, countries, and full positions from leader to cut line.
